The Irish Village · Al Garhoud, Dubai
The IV Campaign
To drive engagement for Dubai's iconic The Irish Village, this campaign turned the venue's shorthand initials — IV — into a clever linguistic hook across social media. By embedding "IV" into everyday copy, we created a playful, typographic signature that gave the brand a distinct visual voice. From highlighting the menu (TraditIoVnal, dIVine) to driving community interaction (TrIVia, VIbes), the concept seamlessly unified food photography and user-generated content under one memorable, witty, and cohesive theme.
