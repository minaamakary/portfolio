Fenty Beauty · Sephora GCC · DSF 2021
The Island by Fenty Skin
As part of the exclusive GCC launch of Rihanna's Fenty Skin at Sephora, the Dubai Mall Promenade was transformed into a fully immersive tropical island pop-up — one of the most ambitious beauty activations ever staged at DSF.
The 10-day experience featured a tiki island resort concept with themed zones named after Fenty Skin products — including the Total Cleans'r Cove, Fat Water Falls, and Hydra Vizor Sundeck. A tropical juice bar, hidden QR codes unlocking prizes, Spotify playlists, and product trial stations made it a social-first, fully shareable experience.
The activation coincided with Rihanna's takeover of the Burj Khalifa facade — her appearance across the world's tallest building driving audiences to the island pop-up below.

The IV Campaign
To drive engagement for Dubai's iconic The Irish Village, this campaign turned the venue's shorthand initials — IV — into a clever linguistic hook across social media. By embedding "IV" into everyday copy, we created a playful, typographic signature that gave the brand a distinct visual voice. From highlighting the menu (TraditIoVnal, dIVine) to driving community interaction (TrIVia, VIbes), the concept seamlessly unified food photography and user-generated content under one memorable, witty, and cohesive theme.
